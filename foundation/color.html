Minimal Color Palette


The portoflio is minimally themed with two primary and one secondary color.

Almost black.
Primary color for all active texts such as headers and paragraphs.

Hex: #333333

Faded black.
Secondary color for all inactive texts in nav bar.

Hex: #333333

Ghost white.
Background color for cards and footer.

Hex: #F6F7FB

Persian blue.
Color for links.

Hex: #072AC8
