Logo

The logo comprises of my italicized initials "HA" inside a solid circle. An exclamatory mark was added by recommendation to make it quirky. Below are the different variations of the logo.


Color

The dark blue in this logo makes it ready for use in web, and print. It also evokes feelings such as passionate, bright, fun and creative.


Black and White

The black and white version of the logo gives a rich premium feeling. It evokes adjectives such as professional, and minimal. This logo can be used for backgrounds where the colored version's dark blue does not fit well.


Reverse

This is a revere version of the black and white logo. It can be used for darker backgrounds.


Fav Icon

This version of the logo can be used as fav icon for browsers.
