Cards

Cards are customizable containers that helps to display content in a crisp and succinct manner. Cards may contain images, gifs or videos. They are often clickable and have an mimic an real world element through shadows and border radius.

Desktop, Tablet,and mobile view

Best practices & Accessibility

  1. Limit content inside the card.
  2. Link all cards to their relevant pages.
  3. Make sure all the cards across the portfolio are of the same size.
  4. Add a hover on box shadow effect to the cards to convey like they are clickable.
  5. Change cursor type to pointer when hovering cursor over cards.

Code

          
        <div class="card" style="width: 18rem;background-color: #f6f7fb;border: none;">

        <div class="card-body">

        <h5 class="card-title">Project title</h5>

        <img class="card-img-top" src="thumbnail.png" alt="Card image cap">

        </div>

        </div>
